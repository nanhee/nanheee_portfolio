Biography

Nanhee In has grown up with art. Her mother has encouraged to study art and supported to go to an art school.
It was in HwangGeum Art School when photography and graphic design became her focus.
When she was younger, she had relied on photographs as a means to preserve memories of events and people.
Her decision to study photography and graphic design at Stony Brook University was a seemingly natural extension of her love of art.
In 2015, Nanhee had a group exhibition for her photography in Stony Brook University.
urrently lives and works in Stony Brook, New York.
